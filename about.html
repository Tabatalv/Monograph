At Monograph Communications, the person behind the clickity-clack of

and clients, including a multibillion-dollar Canadian co-operative, a
burgeoning software startup, and a world-class technology park.

Kim helps businesses talk like a person to their people — customers and
employees, both — with clarity and creativity.
